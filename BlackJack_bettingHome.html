Special rule!

  1. You draw random card number from 1 to 10.
  2. Your total number cannot exceed 21. If you reach 21, you win.
  3. You can stop drawing the card. If you do, the computer will draw the card.
  4. The person with the higher number, but not over 21 will win the game.
  5. You can bet your money in the special match, if you win, you earn your betted money, if you lose, you lose your money.
  6. The computer will keep draw the card until their total becomes larger than the user's.
  7. You will play until one of you score 5 points. Good luck
